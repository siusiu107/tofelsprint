The decisive factor is methodological humility; once that is stated, the passage presents as
the lesson of agricultural revolutions and the reorganization of rural labor: inference is
constrained by what competing models would also predict. Provided that one wishes to move

paired with tests that change the conditions under which it appears. Whereas popular
summaries treat land-use intensification as an endpoint, the passage treats it as a starting

Not the obvious explanation but the apparent regularity of land-use intensification is what

In the end, the passage concludes that the most informative evidence is often the evidence

framework in which land-use intensification is interpreted through explicit boundary
