Ultimately, the passage argues for humility: evidence can rule out stories more easily than
it can confirm them. When later results contradict a prediction, the goal is not to defend a
favored story but to revise the assumptions that produced it.

One advantage of the approach is that it treats uncertainty as information. By reporting

Another takeaway is that replication across settings is most informative when researchers
document what differs between sites, not just what looks similar. In addition, the authors
noted that some variables are easier to measure than to interpret, so they treated proxy
indicators as tentative unless validated independently. In the authors’ view, the best
progress will come from combining multiple methods and then checking whether they converge
